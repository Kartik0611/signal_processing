cummax

Cumulative maximum

Calling Sequence

M = cummax(A)
M = cummax(A,dim)
M = cummax(_,direction)

Parameters

A:

real|complex numbers - vector|matrix Input Array For complex elements, cummax compares the magnitude of elements. If the magnitude are same, phase angles are compared.

Ws:

scalar or vector of length 2, elements must be in the range [0,1]

Rp:

real or complex value

Rs:

real or complex value

Examples

1) Cumulative maximum values in a vector
v = [8 9 1 10 6 1 3 6 10 10]
M = cummax(v)

Expected output: [8 9 9 10 10 10 10 10 10 10]
